Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the "Valeria Vega" application (the "App"), a private tool used exclusively to manage and publish content on social media accounts owned and operated by the App's administrator.

1. Data collection

The App does not collect, store, or process personal data from third-party users. It is not distributed to the public and has no external users. The App only accesses data of the social media accounts owned by its administrator (posts, comments, messages, and insights) through the official Meta APIs, solely to manage those accounts.

2. Data use

Data accessed through the Meta APIs is used exclusively for content publishing, audience analytics, and message management of the administrator's own accounts. No data is sold, shared, or transferred to third parties.

3. Data storage

The App does not maintain databases of user information. Access tokens are stored securely and privately by the administrator.

4. Data deletion

Since the App does not collect data from third-party users, there is no user data to delete. To request deletion of any data related to the administrator's own accounts, or for any privacy inquiry, contact the email below and it will be handled within 30 days.
